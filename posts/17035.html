Your shields were failing, sir

January 1, 2014 by Mark

This should be interesting. Then maybe you should consider this: if anything happens to them, Starfleet is going to want a full investigation. I recommend you don't fire until you're within 40,000 kilometers.

What? We're not at all alike! When has justice ever been as simple as a rule book? In all trust, there is the possibility for betrayal. When has justice ever been as simple as a rule book? We finished our first sensor sweep of the neutral zone. The game's not big enough unless it scares you a little. I'll alert the crew. Mr. Crusher, ready a collision course with the Borg ship. Not if I weaken first. The unexpected is our normal routine. Now we know what they mean by 'advanced' tactical training. I guess it's better to be lucky than good. I'll be sure to note that in my log. I can't. As much as I care about you, my first duty is to the ship. How long can two people talk about nothing? But the probability of making a six is no greater than that of rolling a seven. You enjoyed that. Wait a minute - you've been declared dead.

You can't give orders around here. Sure. You'd be surprised how far a hug goes with Geordi, or Worf. The Enterprise computer system is controlled by three primary main processor cores, cross-linked with a redundant melacortz ramistat, fourteen kiloquad interface modules.

The look in your eyes, I recognize it. You used to have it for me. A lot of things can change in twelve years, Admiral. Did you come here for something in particular or just general Riker-bashing? Computer, belay that order. What's a knock-out like you doing in a computer-generated gin joint like this? This is not about revenge. This is about justice. Yes, absolutely, I do indeed concur, wholeheartedly!
